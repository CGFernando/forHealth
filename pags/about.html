Fernando Guimarães Cavatão

Graduated in pharmacy from the Centro Universitário da Fundação Educacional de Barretos (UNIFEB) in 2016. Postgraduate degree in Clinical Analysis with an emphasis on Molecular Biology, from the Residency Program in Clinical Analysis at the Hospital de Clínicas de Porto Alegre (HCPA), 2022. During the postgraduate studies, I worked with molecular diagnosis of viruses, bacteria, fungi, protozoa, mutations and chromosomal translocations related to leukemia and myelodysplasia. Also, worked in the HCPA's hematology unit, analyzing blood slides, releasing blood counts, and performing hemostasis tests. Moreover, worked in the Flow Cytometry Unit of HCPA with sample marking, cytometer acquisition, interpretation of lymphocyte subpopulations, and quantification of CD3 and CD4. In the same laboratory, I worked in the Special Techniques Sector with protein electrophoresis, immunofixation, and therapeutic drug monitoring by HPLC. Master's degree from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS), Cellular and Molecular Biology Program.
